01 / The Eduba frame

Three slices. Most of the value is not in the ten percent everyone talks about.

60%

Plain code and databases

A shared footage library across the seven brands. A routing system between Motionize sites and post-production. A clean handoff between Video.Miami, MVP, Boatopia, and the core agency. This is the layer most teams skip, because it does not sound like AI. It is where the hours come back.

30%

Rule-based logic

Pricing tiers for the mid-market project band. Scope validation before a shoot gets booked. Assignment rules for which cinematographer runs yacht work versus corporate interviews. Deterministic, explainable, cheaper to run than a model call.

10%

Genuine AI work

For Bonomotion, two places earn the model. The computer vision behind Motionize time-lapse. The automated edit and translation layer for corporate explainer work. Everything else on the AI menu sits closer to orchestration than to research.

03 / The framework under the work

Interpretable Context Methodology

Seven brands. 3,500+ past productions. A Motionize product plus an agency delivery pipeline. These are context-organization problems before they are AI problems.

ICM organizes agent context as a layered filesystem, L0 through L4, with measurable reproducibility gains. Published to ACM TiiS. Useful when you want seven brands findable, a footage archive searchable, and a product team that can onboard without a founder sitting next to them.
